Earn Ethereum Farming Squirrel!

Ether Squirrel Farm is the #1 squirrel farming simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more squirrel you have, the more nuts they lay (each squirrel lays at a rate of 1 per day). Hatch more squirrel with your nuts to multiply your production, or cash them out for Ethereum!

Powered by Metamask

This game requires Metamask

Squirrels Are Frail Technology

To prevent inflation, Squirrel Farms have several tricks over their competitors. Squirrels are attached to their progeny and one third of your nest will die if you sell your nuts. Those willing to make the greatest sacrifice can become the Owl, and collect tribute on every nut sale.
